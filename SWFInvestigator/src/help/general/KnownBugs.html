Known Bugs/Limitations

  • The LSO Navigator does not work for localhost or LocalWithNet LSOs.
  • The LSO Navigator can not handle LSOs that contain AMF pointers.
  • The LSO Navigator can not handle LSOs that are in directories with a "#" in their name.
  • The LSO Navigator can not open Shared objects if "Allow third party content to store data on your computer" is not set within the Flash settings manager.
  • The LSO Navigator sometimes encounters Error 2134. This may be because the LSO is in use or due to a race condition.
  • The SWFIntruder functionality may have trouble with SWFs that expect to be the topmost SWF.
  • Hex Editor may be slow when handling larger files.
  • Some utilities such as AS3Compiler won't work if you open multiple AS3Compiler windows. Only one can exist at a time.
  • Set wmode to opaque in order to prevent SWF content overlaying drop down menus.
  • DefineFont3 does not always parse correctly
